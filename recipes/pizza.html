Pizza Salami

Description

This classic pizza salami is a simple yet delicious dish that is sure to satisfy any pizza lover. Featuring a crispy, thin crust topped with rich tomato sauce, gooey melted cheese, and generous slices of spicy salami, it’s a perfect balance of flavors.

The salami adds a savory, slightly smoky kick, while the herbs and fresh basil garnish bring an aromatic touch to each slice. Whether for a family dinner or a casual gathering, this pizza is always a hit!

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 12-15 slices of salami
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional for garnish)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C) and prepare a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness and transfer it to the baking sheet or stone.
  3. Brush the dough with olive oil, then spread the tomato sauce evenly over the surface, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce, ensuring even coverage.
  5. Lay the salami slices on top of the cheese, spacing them evenly across the pizza.
  6. Sprinkle dried oregano, basil, salt, and pepper over the pizza for added flavor.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling.
  8. Remove from the oven and let the pizza cool for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh basil leaves if desired, then slice and serve hot!
